01 · Welcome

Click any UI element. See where it lives.

Inspekt turns your browser into an X-ray for components. Click a button, card, or nav item — Inspekt shows the source file, the component name, and the surrounding lines of code.

03 · Two ways to use it

On your projects, or on any site.

Inspekt works everywhere, but it really shines wired into your own project's build tool.

Best experience
On your own projects

Add the Inspekt plugin to your build tool (Vite, Webpack, Rspack, esbuild). Click anything on your running app — jump straight to the source in your editor, and snippets ship with each agent grab.

On any other website

Click the Inspekt icon in the Chrome toolbar. Click elements to explore their DOM structure. Source-jumping is disabled (the site doesn't have the breadcrumbs baked in), but you can still poke around.

04 · Pick your editor

Where should "Open in editor" go?

When you click an element on your project, Inspekt opens its source file in the editor you pick here. You can change this anytime in Settings.
