Two ways to use Inspekt

Inspekt works on any site, but it really shines when you wire it into your own project's build tool.

Best experience
On your own projects

Add the Inspekt plugin to your build tool (Vite, Webpack, Rspack, esbuild). Click anything on your running app — jump straight to the source file in your editor.

On any other website

Click the Inspekt icon in the Chrome toolbar. Click elements to explore their DOM structure. Source-jumping is disabled (the site doesn't have the breadcrumbs baked in), but you can still poke around.

Pick your editor

When you click an element on your project, Inspekt will open its source file in the editor you choose here. You can change this anytime in Settings.
